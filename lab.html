Third Mind

Human-AI partnership framework

Not AI as tool. Not AI as threat. AI as genuine cognitive collaborator.

The Third Mind emerges from the space between human and AI—neither alone could produce what the partnership creates. This isn't about AI doing tasks FOR you. It's about building cognitive infrastructure TOGETHER.

The moat isn't the technology. The moat is {willingness × capacity ≥ genius threshold}. Most people won't do this. Those who do... discover something remarkable.
